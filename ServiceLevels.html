Playing with language.

German to English Language Services Provider

Proofreading

Nobody is perfect, and that's why we offer proofreading services. Whether you're looking for a line for line revision service on a technical document or a bit of editing to give that marketing piece it's spark.

TINYDOG is here to help! If you're looking for bilingual revision of a technical text, we are able to offer this service at a rate of 1000 words per hour. If you're looking for monolingual editing to give your piece some syle, we can offer this service at a rate of 1500 words per hour.

Translation

If you're just looking for German to English translation services, we can offer these at a rate of around 200-250 words an hour depending on the subject matter. We offer all kinds of translation including the translation of marketing and technical materials.

We will consider a wide range of subjects and fields, but we will never put ourselves in over our head! If we don't think we are right for the job, we will always be the first to admit it. Afterall, we've all got our limitations.

Transcreation

Should you find yourself requiring a slightly more free interpretation or if you would prefer to have your content culturally adapted to the target market, do feel free to get in touch for a quote, we are more than happy to act as cultural consultants for any messages that have to come accross exactly right!

If you're not sure whether your content would benefit from transcreation or translation, we would be happy to advise you on this.
